What is Cup Of Excellence?

The original Cup of Excellence is an annual competition held in several countries to identify the highest quality coffees produced. Here at Kenzan, our Cup of Excellence is a coffee cupping competition with some of Denver's best micro roasters. We have sampled coffees from Boxcar, Corvus, Huckleberry, ink!, Middle State, and Sweet Bloom coffee roasters.

How Do We Rate Our Coffee's?

We will be doing our rating through a coffee cupping. A coffee cupping is a standardized way of comparing coffees side by side. In our cupping we will smell and taste the following coffee types and note our ratings and thoughts on the given score sheet. To help us identify specific flavors we will use a taster's wheel that lays out various flavors and profiles.

Steps

  • 1) Note aroma from the dry coffee grounds
  • 2) Note aroma from the wet coffee grounds
  • 3) Taste the brewed coffee for
    • Acidity
    • Body
    • Flavour
    • Finish
